First-Time Packing List

My curated list for Indian students moving to Germany.

Essential Documents

  • Original Passport + 5 Photocopies
  • University Admission Letter & Enrollment Proof
  • Blocked Account Confirmation
  • Biometric Photos (Carry at least 15-20)

Kitchen & Spices

  • Pressure Cooker (Small/Medium)
  • Basic Spices (Turmeric, Chilli, Garam Masala - 500g each)
  • Rolling Pin (Belan) & Tawa

Clothing

  • At least 1 Heavy Winter Jacket (Buy rest in Germany)
  • Formal Suit for Interviews
  • Good Quality Thermal Wear (2 pairs)

*Tip: Don't overpack clothes. Germany has better winter gear for the local weather.
